== Installation ==

Simply follow these steps to install
1. Unzip the file.
1. Upload the "woo-beanstream-payment.zip" folder to your WordPress Plugins directory.
1. Login to your WordPress Admin, then go to Plugins and activate the "Woo Beanstream Hosted Payment Getway" plugin
1. Within the WordPress Admin, go to WooCommerce >> Settings, then click on the Payment Gateways tab, then click on the Beanstream link.
1. Enter the proper information...
